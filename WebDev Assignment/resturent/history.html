Our History

Our passion is great food, served with a good wine, cold beer or a coffee, served in a beautiful space with great music playing. We change our menus seasonally to incorporate fresh produce from our local suppliers. Passionate about wine, we work with some of the best small producers and importers who never fail to impress us with their ability to seek out the best wines of the season, keeping our menu fresh & exciting. We take care of this famous Joycean building, which we renovated in 2007 with loving care. Our music playlist has some great classics, indie favourites, a lot of Bowie and the best of emerging new talent. So, if it’s a lazy breakfast come brunch, lunch with friends, dinner with family or a special occasion, join us in this historic building and we will treat you to great music, friendly staff and above all great food.
